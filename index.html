A quieter place to meet yourself

Moonspace is an online therapy practice created for people who feel emotionally overwhelmed, mentally tired, or quietly disconnected from themselves.

Therapy here is not rushed. There is no pressure to explain everything perfectly or arrive with clarity. This is a space where confusion, uncertainty, and pauses are welcome.

Moonspace offers calm, reflective online counselling for adults across India — grounded in psychological depth, emotional safety, and respect for your pace.

Why Moonspace exists

Many people function well on the outside while carrying anxiety, exhaustion, self-doubt, or emotional pain internally. Modern life often leaves little space to slow down, reflect, or feel understood without being offered immediate solutions.

Moonspace was created in response to this. It exists for people who feel stretched thin, emotionally cluttered, or unsure how to articulate what feels off — yet sense that something within them needs attention.

This practice values slowness over urgency, depth over performance, and understanding over fixing. There is no expectation to be productive in therapy. Growth here can be subtle, quiet, and deeply personal.

The name Moonspace reflects this philosophy. Like the moon, which moves through phases without rushing or demanding consistency, this space honours emotional cycles, inner contradictions, and gradual change.

How therapy is approached

Therapy at Moonspace is collaborative and relational rather than prescriptive. Sessions are shaped by careful listening and attunement, not rigid frameworks or timelines.

Psychological safety is prioritised before insight. This means respecting emotional boundaries, pacing conversations thoughtfully, and remaining sensitive to how experiences are held in the body and nervous system.

The work draws from established psychological frameworks while remaining flexible to individual needs. Emphasis is placed on understanding emotional patterns, internal narratives, and relational dynamics — not simply managing symptoms.

Therapy here is not about being fixed. It is about developing awareness, self-compassion, and agency, so you can move through life with greater clarity and trust in yourself.

Online counselling services

Moonspace offers individual online counselling sessions for adults across India. Sessions are conducted via secure video calls and are fully confidential.

Therapy may support concerns related to anxiety, chronic stress, burnout, emotional regulation, overthinking, identity questions, relational difficulties, and periods of transition.

Sessions are personalised rather than agenda-driven. What is explored emerges from what feels most relevant and present for you.

Your therapist

Moonspace is led by Girisha Kamboj, a psychologist who values depth, nuance, and ethical care over quick interventions.

Her work is grounded in empathy, curiosity, and respect for each individual’s lived experience. Training and clinical education have emphasised not only psychological theory, but also self-reflection, boundaries, and responsibility.

Girisha works particularly well with individuals navigating anxiety, emotional overwhelm, burnout, identity concerns, and relational patterns.

Above all, she believes therapy should feel human — spacious enough for uncertainty, complexity, and slow growth.
